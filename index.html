Projects

Microwave Wideband Synthesizer (900 MHz - 13 GHz)

  • Utilized Analog Devices ADF4368 PLL, with an AVR microcontroller automating configuration at startup via SPI and EEPROM data.
  • Designed with a Python-based GUI for easy configuration and USB interface for user control.
  • Offers single frequency and frequency step operation modes, with fine-tuning available via the fractional-N synthesizer.
  • Achieved ultralow phase noise of -120 dBc/Hz at 12.8 GHz with a 10 kHz offset.
  • Provides a flexible, low-cost alternative to traditional DRO solutions for various RF applications, generating a clean sine wave over a wide frequency range.

Delay Locked Loop with TSMC 65 nm PDK (1 GHz)

  • Designed and implemented a delay-locked loop (DLL) using TSMC 65 nm PDK in Cadence Virtuoso.
  • Developed key components (PFD, charge pump, VCDL) in Verilog-A for behavioral simulation.
  • Achieved fast lock time of 20 ns and verified the design with schematic simulation in TSMC 65 nm.
  • Completed clean layout with no DRC and LVS violations.
  • Simulated and verified DLL operation using test cases with control voltages of 0V and 1V, ensuring phase alignment and delay control.

Single Balanced RF Mixer with Colpitts Oscillator (4 MHz)

  • Colpitts oscillator produces 16V peak-to-peak output from only 5V power supply.
  • NMOS differential pair loaded with RF chokes for additional gain and filtering.
  • Effective filtering removes upper sideband (USB) and higher order harmonics.
